The Captain's Charter

The sea is yours to wander. There is no failing — only what you discover before you turn for port.

Sailing

  • Touch: drag the wheel (bottom-left) — the further you push, the more sail you set.
  • Keyboard: W/↑ thrust, AD/←→ steer, S ease off.

Discovery

  • Your compass needle leans toward the nearest unclaimed island.
  • Slow down beside an island and the DOCK button rises. Open the chest for gold — and sometimes an Echo, a vision the deep offers up.
  • Salvage drifting crates, and ride out the squalls that roll across the water.

Port

  • Return to port anytime from the pause menu to bank your voyage and chase your best.
